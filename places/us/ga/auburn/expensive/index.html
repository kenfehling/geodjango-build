Home prices

Auburn's median home cost is $142,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $68,140 (32%) lower. It is roughly $75,500 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $148,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Auburn is $864. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $213 (19%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $198 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $289 (25%) lower.
