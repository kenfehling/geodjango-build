In comparison to the surrounding areas, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Savannah however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home cost is $207,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $93,500 (31%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $10,200 (4%) lower, and is approximately $50,800 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Montgomery the median rent is $931 a month. This is approximately $340 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $131 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $88 (8%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
