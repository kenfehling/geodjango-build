In comparison to nearby areas, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Savannah however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home cost is $207,300. It is about $93,500 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $10,200 (4%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $50,800 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Montgomery the median monthly rent is $931. It is approximately $340 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $131 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $88 (8%) lower.
