In comparison to its nearby areas, Montgomery is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home price is $207,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $93,500 (31%) lower.It is approximately $10,200 (4%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $50,800 (32%) higher.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $931. It is approximately $340 (26%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $131 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $88 (8%) lower.
