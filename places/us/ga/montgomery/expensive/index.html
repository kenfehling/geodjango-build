Compared to its surrounding areas, Montgomery is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Savannahhowever, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery costs $207,300. It is approximately $93,500 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $10,200 (4%) lower, and it is approximately $50,800 (32%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery is $931 per month. This is approximately $340 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $131 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah is roughly $88 (8%) lower.
