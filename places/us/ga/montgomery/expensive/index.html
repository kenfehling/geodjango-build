In comparison to other places in this area, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Savannah, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $207,300. It is approximately $93,500 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $10,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $50,800 (32%) higher.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $931. This is about $340 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $131 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $88 (8%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
