Compared to other places in this area, Fort Oglethorpe is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Huntsville however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Oglethorpe is $128,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,840 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $89,400 (41%) lower, and is roughly $54,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Fort Oglethorpe's median monthly rent is $759. It is approximately $128 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $303 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $68 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
