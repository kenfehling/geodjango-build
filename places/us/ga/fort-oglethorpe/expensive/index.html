Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Oglethorpe is $128,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $16,840 (15%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $89,400 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $54,800 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Oglethorpe the median rent is $759 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $128 (14%) lower. It is approximately $303 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $68 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
