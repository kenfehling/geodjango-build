Compared to its nearby areas, Fort Oglethorpe is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Huntsville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Oglethorpe is valued at $128,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,840 (15%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $89,400 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $54,800 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Oglethorpe the median rent is $759 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $128 (14%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $303 (28%) lower, and is roughly $68 (8%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
