Compared to its surrounding areas, Fort Oglethorpe is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Huntsville however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Fort Oglethorpe's median home price is $128,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $16,840 (15%) higher.It is approximately $89,400 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $54,800 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Oglethorpe the median monthly rent is $759. This is approximately $128 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $303 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $68 (8%) lower.
