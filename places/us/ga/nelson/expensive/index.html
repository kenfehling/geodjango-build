Home prices

Nelson's median home cost is $176,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $16,200 (8%) lower. It is roughly $41,200 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $114,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Nelson the median rent is $908 a month. It is roughly $81 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $245 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
