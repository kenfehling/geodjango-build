Home prices

The median home in Summerville is valued at $54,900. It is roughly $30,400 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $162,600 (74%) lower, and is approximately $235,500 (81%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Summerville is $507 per month. This is about $85 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $555 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $646 (56%) lower.
