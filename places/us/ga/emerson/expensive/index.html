Home prices

The price of the median home in Emerson is $158,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $3,900 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $59,400 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $132,300 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Emerson the median rent is $853 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $185 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $209 (19%) lower, and it is about $300 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
