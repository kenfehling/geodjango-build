Home prices

The cost of the median home in Reynolds is $65,600. This is approximately $9,160 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $151,900 (69%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $224,800 (77%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Reynolds is $358. It is roughly $142 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $704 (66%) lower, and it is about $795 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
