Home prices

The median home cost in Brunswick is $108,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $57,080 (34%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $108,900 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $64,600 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Brunswick is $729 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $141 (16%) lower.It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $336 (31%) lower.
