In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brunswick is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brunswick is $108,600. This is approximately $57,080 (34%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $108,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $64,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Brunswick's median rent is $729 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $141 (16%) lower. It is about $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $336 (31%) lower.
