Home prices

Brunswick's median home price is $108,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $57,080 (34%) lower.It is roughly $108,900 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $64,600 (37%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Brunswick the median monthly rent is $729. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $141 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $333 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $336 (31%) lower.
