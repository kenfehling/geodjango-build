Compared to other places in this area, Johns Creek is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Atlanta, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Johns Creek is $400,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $50,520 (14%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $182,600 (83%) higher, and it is about $109,700 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Johns Creek the median monthly rent is $1,677. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $216 (14%) higher. It is about $615 (57%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $524 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
