Compared to nearby areas, Johns Creek is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Johns Creek costs $400,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $50,520 (14%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $182,600 (83%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $109,700 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Johns Creek the median monthly rent is $1,677. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $216 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $615 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $524 (45%) higher.
