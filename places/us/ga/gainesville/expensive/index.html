Compared to its surrounding areas, Gainesville is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gainesville is valued at $219,300. It is about $48,660 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and is roughly $71,100 (24%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Gainesville the median monthly rent is $941. This is roughly $44 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $121 (11%) lower, and it is about $212 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
