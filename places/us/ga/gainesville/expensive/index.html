In comparison to its surrounding areas, Gainesville is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Gainesville's median home price is $219,300. This is approximately $48,660 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is is very similar in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $71,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Gainesville the median monthly rent is $941. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $44 (4%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $121 (11%) lower, and is about $212 (18%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
