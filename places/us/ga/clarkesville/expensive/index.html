Compared to nearby areas, Clarkesville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Clarkesville costs $142,900. This is about $30,280 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $74,600 (34%) lower, and is about $147,500 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clarkesville is $694. It is approximately $36 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $368 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $459 (39%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
