Home prices

The median home in Clarkesville costs $142,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30,280 (26%) higher. It is approximately $74,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $147,500 (50%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Clarkesville the median rent is $694 per month. This is approximately $36 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $368 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $459 (39%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
