Home prices

The median home in Clarkesville is valued at $142,900. It is about $30,280 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $74,600 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $147,500 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Clarkesville the median rent is $694 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $36 (4%) lower.It is roughly $368 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $459 (39%) lower.
