Home prices

The median home price in Clarkesville is $142,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $30,280 (26%) higher.It is approximately $74,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $147,500 (50%) lower.

Rents

Clarkesville's median monthly rent is $694. It is roughly $36 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $368 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $459 (39%) lower.
