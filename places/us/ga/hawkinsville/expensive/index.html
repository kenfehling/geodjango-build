Home prices

The median home in Hawkinsville costs $139,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $57,380 (70%) higher. It is roughly $78,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $2,100 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Hawkinsville is $715 per month. This is roughly $184 (34%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $347 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $191 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
