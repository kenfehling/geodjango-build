In comparison to other places in this area, Byron is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Byron is valued at $164,100. This is approximately $41,580 (33%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $53,400 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $126,300 (43%) lower.

Rents

Byron's median monthly rent is $768. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $77 (9%) lower. It is roughly $294 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $385 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
