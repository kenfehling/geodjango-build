Home prices

Byron's median home cost is $164,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $41,580 (33%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $53,400 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $126,300 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Byron the median monthly rent is $768. This is approximately $77 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $294 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $385 (33%) lower.
