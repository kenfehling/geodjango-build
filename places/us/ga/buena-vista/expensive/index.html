Home prices

Rents

In Buena Vista the median monthly rent is $377. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $90 (19%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $685 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $529 (58%) lower.
