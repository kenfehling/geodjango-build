Home prices

The cost of the median home in Buena Vista is $109,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $40,940 (59%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $108,100 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $31,900 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Buena Vista is $377 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $90 (19%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $685 (64%) lower, and it is approximately $529 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
