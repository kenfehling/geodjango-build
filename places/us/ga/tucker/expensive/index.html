Compared to the surrounding areas, Tucker is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Tucker costs $256,200. This is about $97,740 (61%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $38,700 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $34,200 (11%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Tucker the median rent is $1,075 a month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $78 (6%) lower.
