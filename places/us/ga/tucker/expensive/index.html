Home prices

Rents

Tucker's median rent is $1,075 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is approximately $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $78 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
