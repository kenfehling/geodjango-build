Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tybee Island is $503,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $185,920 (58%) higher. It is approximately $286,200 (131%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $347,200 (221%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Tybee Island is $1,208 per month. This is roughly $94 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $146 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is about $189 (18%) higher.
