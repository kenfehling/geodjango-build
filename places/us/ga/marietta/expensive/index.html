In comparison to other places in this area, Marietta is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Marietta is valued at $287,600. This is roughly $23,920 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $70,100 (32%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Marietta the median rent is $1,102 per month. It is about $143 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $40 (3%) higher, and is about $51 (4%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
