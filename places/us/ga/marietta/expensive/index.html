Compared to the surrounding areas, Marietta is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Marietta is $287,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $23,920 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $70,100 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is is very similar.

Rents

Marietta's median monthly rent is $1,102. This is about $143 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $40 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $51 (4%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
