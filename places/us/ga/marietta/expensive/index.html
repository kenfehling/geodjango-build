Compared to nearby areas, Marietta is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Marietta costs $287,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $23,920 (7%) lower.It is about $70,100 (32%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is is very similar.

Rents

Marietta's median rent is $1,102 per month. This is roughly $143 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $40 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $51 (4%) lower.
