Home prices

Marietta's median home price is $287,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $23,920 (7%) lower. It is about $70,100 (32%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Marietta the median rent is $1,102 a month. It is about $143 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $40 (3%) higher, and it is about $51 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
