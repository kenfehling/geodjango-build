Compared to the surrounding areas, Marietta is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marietta is $287,600. This is approximately $23,920 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $70,100 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marietta is $1,102. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $143 (11%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $40 (3%) higher, and is approximately $51 (4%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
