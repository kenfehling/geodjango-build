Home prices

The median home price in North Decatur is $295,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $75,040 (20%) lower.It is roughly $78,300 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $5,400 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Decatur is $1,205 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $14 (1%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $143 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $52 (4%) higher.
