In comparison to nearby areas, North Decatur is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's close to average.

Home prices

North Decatur's median home price is $295,800. It is roughly $75,040 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $78,300 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $5,400 (1%) higher.

Rents

North Decatur's median monthly rent is $1,205. This is about $14 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $143 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $52 (4%) higher.
