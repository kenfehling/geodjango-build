Home prices

The value of the median home in North Decatur is $295,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $75,040 (20%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $78,300 (36%) higher, and is approximately $5,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in North Decatur is $1,205 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $143 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $52 (4%) higher.
