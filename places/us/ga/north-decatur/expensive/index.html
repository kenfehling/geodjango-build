Compared to the surrounding areas, North Decatur is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Decatur is $295,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $75,040 (20%) lower. It is roughly $78,300 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $5,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

North Decatur's median monthly rent is $1,205. This is approximately $14 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $143 (13%) higher, and it is approximately $52 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
