Home prices

The median home in North Decatur is valued at $295,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $75,040 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $78,300 (36%) higher, and it is about $5,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In North Decatur the median rent is $1,205 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $14 (1%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $143 (13%) higher, and it is approximately $52 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
