Home prices

The value of the median home in Montrose is $91,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $6,760 (7%) higher.It is approximately $126,200 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County it is roughly $28,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Montrose the median monthly rent is $521. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $116 (18%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $541 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $297 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
