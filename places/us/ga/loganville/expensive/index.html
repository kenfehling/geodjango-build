Home prices

The price of the median home in Loganville is $202,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $13,860 (7%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $15,200 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $88,100 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Loganville's median monthly rent is $1,176. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $114 (10%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $23 (1%) higher.
