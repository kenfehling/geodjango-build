In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lyons is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lyons is $73,200. This is about $15,360 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $144,300 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is roughly $83,300 (53%) lower.

Rents

Lyons's median monthly rent is $543. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $70 (11%) lower. It is about $519 (48%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $476 (46%) lower.
