Home prices

The median home in Lyons costs $73,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $15,360 (17%) lower. It is roughly $144,300 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Savannah it is roughly $83,300 (53%) lower.

Rents

Lyons's median monthly rent is $543. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $70 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $519 (48%) lower, and is about $476 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
