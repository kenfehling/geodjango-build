Compared to its surrounding areas, Lyons is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Savannah, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lyons is $73,200. This is roughly $15,360 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $144,300 (66%) lower, and is roughly $83,300 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Lyons is $543 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $70 (11%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $519 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $476 (46%) lower.
