In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lyons is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

Lyons's median home cost is $73,200. This is about $15,360 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $144,300 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $83,300 (53%) lower.

Rents

Lyons's median monthly rent is $543. It is approximately $70 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $519 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $476 (46%) lower.
