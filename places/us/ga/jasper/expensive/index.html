Rents

In Jasper the median monthly rent is $761. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $186 (19%) lower. It is approximately $301 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $392 (33%) lower.
