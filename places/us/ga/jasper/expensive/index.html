Home prices

The median home in Jasper is valued at $184,300. This is roughly $41,620 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $33,200 (15%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $106,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

Jasper's median monthly rent is $761. This is about $186 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $301 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $392 (33%) lower.
