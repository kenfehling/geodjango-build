Home prices

The median home in Martinez is valued at $162,600. It is about $6,400 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $54,900 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $18,500 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Martinez is $1,087. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $81 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $25 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbia is about $154 (16%) higher.
