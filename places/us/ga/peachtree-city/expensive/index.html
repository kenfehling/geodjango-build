Home prices

The value of the median home in Peachtree City is $338,900. This is roughly $94,740 (38%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $121,400 (55%) higher, and is about $48,500 (16%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Peachtree City's median rent is $1,408 a month. This is roughly $369 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $346 (32%) higher, and is approximately $255 (22%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
