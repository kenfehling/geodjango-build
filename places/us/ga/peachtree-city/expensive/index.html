In comparison to the surrounding areas, Peachtree City is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Peachtree City is $338,900. This is about $94,740 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $121,400 (55%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $48,500 (16%) higher.

Rents

Peachtree City's median monthly rent is $1,408. It is about $369 (35%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $346 (32%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $255 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
