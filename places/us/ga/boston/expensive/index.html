Home prices

The median home in Boston is valued at $92,300. This is approximately $8,420 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $125,200 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $111,500 (54%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median rent in Boston is $633 per month. It is roughly $44 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $429 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Tallahassee it is about $390 (38%) lower.
