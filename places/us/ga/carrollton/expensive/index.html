Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Carrollton is $845 a month. It is roughly $86 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $217 (20%) lower, and is roughly $308 (26%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
