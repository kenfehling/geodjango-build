In comparison to its surrounding areas, Carrollton is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Carrollton is $155,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $29,140 (23%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $62,200 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $135,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Carrollton the median monthly rent is $845. It is roughly $86 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $217 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $308 (26%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
