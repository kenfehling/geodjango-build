Home prices

The median home in Carlton is valued at $153,500. This is about $40,780 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $64,000 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $136,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

Carlton's median rent is $691 per month. This is about $81 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $371 (34%) lower, and is approximately $462 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
