In comparison to other places in this area, Leesburg is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Leesburg is $116,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $27,260 (30%) higher.It is roughly $100,600 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $24,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leesburg is $997. This is approximately $301 (43%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $65 (6%) lower, and it is about $91 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
