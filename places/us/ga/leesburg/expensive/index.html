In comparison to the surrounding areas, Leesburg is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

Leesburg's median home cost is $116,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $27,260 (30%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $100,600 (46%) lower, and is roughly $24,400 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $997. This is approximately $301 (43%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $65 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $91 (10%) higher.
