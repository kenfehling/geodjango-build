Compared to the surrounding areas, Forest Park is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Forest Park is valued at $67,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $28,220 (29%) lower.It is roughly $150,400 (69%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $223,300 (76%) lower.

Rents

In Forest Park the median monthly rent is $866. This is roughly $71 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $196 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $287 (24%) lower.
