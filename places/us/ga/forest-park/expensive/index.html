In comparison to its surrounding areas, Forest Park is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Forest Park is $67,100. It is approximately $28,220 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $150,400 (69%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $223,300 (76%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Forest Park is $866. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $71 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $196 (18%) lower, and is approximately $287 (24%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
