In comparison to its nearby areas, Decatur is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Decatur is $521,900. This is approximately $188,320 (56%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $304,400 (139%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $231,500 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Decatur the median monthly rent is $1,015. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $200 (16%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $47 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $138 (11%) lower.
