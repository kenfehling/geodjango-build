Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $521,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $188,320 (56%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $304,400 (139%) higher, and is about $231,500 (79%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Decatur the median rent is $1,015 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $200 (16%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $47 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $138 (11%) lower.
