Rents

The median rent in Sky Valley is $925 per month. It is approximately $222 (31%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $137 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $80 (9%) higher.
