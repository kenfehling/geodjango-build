Compared to the surrounding areas, Sky Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Sky Valley is valued at $241,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $23,240 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $24,200 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $105,400 (77%) higher.

Rents

Sky Valley's median rent is $925 a month. It is about $222 (31%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $137 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $80 (9%) higher.
