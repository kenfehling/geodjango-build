Home prices

The median home in Blue Ridge costs $167,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $48,480 (40%) higher. It is approximately $49,600 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $122,500 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Blue Ridge the median monthly rent is $670. This is about $18 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $392 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $483 (41%) lower.
