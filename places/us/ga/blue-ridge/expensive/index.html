Home prices

The median home in Blue Ridge is valued at $167,900. This is approximately $48,480 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $49,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $122,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Blue Ridge the median rent is $670 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $18 (2%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $483 (41%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
