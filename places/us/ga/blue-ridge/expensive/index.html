Home prices

The median home in Blue Ridge is valued at $167,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48,480 (40%) higher. It is about $49,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $122,500 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blue Ridge is $670. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $18 (2%) higher. It is about $392 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $483 (41%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
