Home prices

The median home cost in Stonecrest is $126,400. It is about $30,840 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $91,100 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $164,000 (56%) lower.

Rents

Stonecrest's median rent is $1,120 per month. It is approximately $197 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $58 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $33 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
