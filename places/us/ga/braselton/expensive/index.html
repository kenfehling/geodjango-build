Home prices

The median home in Braselton costs $318,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $132,100 (70%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $101,200 (46%) higher, and is approximately $28,300 (9%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Braselton is $1,256 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $298 (31%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $194 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $103 (8%) higher.
