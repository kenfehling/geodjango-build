Home prices

The price of the median home in Indian Springs is $144,700. It is roughly $32,120 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $72,800 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $38,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

Indian Springs's median monthly rent is $1,230. This is approximately $425 (52%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $168 (15%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is approximately $403 (48%) higher.
