In comparison to its surrounding areas, Louisville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Louisville costs $77,500. It is about $12,480 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $140,000 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County is roughly $42,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

Louisville's median rent is $530 a month. It is roughly $48 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $532 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County is about $288 (35%) lower.
