Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Louisville is $530 a month. It is approximately $48 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $532 (50%) lower, and is about $288 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
