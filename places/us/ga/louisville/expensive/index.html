Home prices

The value of the median home in Louisville is $77,500. This is about $12,480 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $140,000 (64%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County is roughly $42,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Louisville the median rent is $530 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $48 (8%) lower. It is approximately $532 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $288 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
