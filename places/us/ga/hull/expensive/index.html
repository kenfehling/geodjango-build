Home prices

The median home price in Hull is $180,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $46,300 (34%) higher. It is roughly $37,500 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $110,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Hull's median rent is $638 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $186 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $424 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $515 (44%) lower.
