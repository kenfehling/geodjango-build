Home prices

Bremen's median home value is $177,300. This is about $63,780 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $40,200 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $113,100 (38%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bremen is $631. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $106 (14%) lower. It is about $431 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $522 (45%) lower.
