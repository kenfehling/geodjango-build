In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bremen is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bremen costs $177,300. This is about $63,780 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $40,200 (18%) lower, and is about $113,100 (38%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bremen is $631. It is about $106 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $431 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $522 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
