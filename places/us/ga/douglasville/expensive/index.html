In comparison to the surrounding areas, Douglasville is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Douglasville costs $171,600. This is approximately $16,180 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $45,900 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $118,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Douglasville the median monthly rent is $1,083. It is approximately $60 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $21 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $70 (6%) lower.
