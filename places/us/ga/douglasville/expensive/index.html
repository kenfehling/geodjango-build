Home prices

Douglasville's median home cost is $171,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $16,180 (10%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $45,900 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $118,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Douglasville the median rent is $1,083 a month. This is about $60 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $21 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $70 (6%) lower.
