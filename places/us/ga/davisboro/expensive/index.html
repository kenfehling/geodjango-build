Home prices

The median home in Davisboro costs $52,900. This is about $18,020 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $164,600 (75%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $67,300 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Rents

The median rent in Davisboro is $713 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $108 (17%) higher. It is approximately $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County it is about $105 (12%) lower.
