Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Graham is $656 a month. This is roughly $86 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $406 (38%) lower, and is approximately $363 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
