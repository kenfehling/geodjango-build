Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Ludowici is $852 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $67 (8%) higher. It is roughly $210 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $213 (19%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
