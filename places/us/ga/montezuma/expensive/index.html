Home prices

The median home in Montezuma is valued at $78,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $15,040 (23%) higher. It is roughly $139,200 (64%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $63,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Montezuma the median rent is $597 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $125 (26%) higher. It is approximately $465 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $309 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
