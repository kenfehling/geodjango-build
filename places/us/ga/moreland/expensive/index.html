Compared to the surrounding areas, Moreland is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Moreland costs $164,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $31,460 (23%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $52,700 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $125,600 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Moreland is $1,167 a month. It is about $263 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $105 (9%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $14 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
