Rents

In Moreland the median monthly rent is $1,167. It is roughly $263 (29%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $105 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $14 (1%) higher.
