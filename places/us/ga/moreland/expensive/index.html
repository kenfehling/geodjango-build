Home prices

The median home in Moreland costs $164,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31,460 (23%) higher.It is roughly $52,700 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $125,600 (43%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Moreland the median monthly rent is $1,167. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $263 (29%) higher.It is approximately $105 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $14 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
