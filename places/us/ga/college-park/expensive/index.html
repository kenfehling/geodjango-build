In comparison to its nearby areas, College Park is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

College Park's median home cost is $183,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $63,720 (53%) higher.It is about $33,800 (15%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $106,700 (36%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

College Park's median monthly rent is $888. This is about $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $174 (16%) lower, and it is about $265 (22%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
