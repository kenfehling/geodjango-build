Compared to nearby areas, Chickamauga is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Chickamauga is $138,200. This is approximately $18,140 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $79,300 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $44,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Chickamauga the median rent is $729 a month. This is approximately $102 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $98 (11%) lower.
