Compared to its nearby areas, Chickamauga is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chickamauga is valued at $138,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $18,140 (11%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $79,300 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $44,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Chickamauga is $729 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $102 (12%) lower.It is roughly $333 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $98 (11%) lower.
