Compared to its surrounding areas, Chickamauga is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Chickamauga is $138,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $18,140 (11%) lower.It is approximately $79,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $44,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

Chickamauga's median rent is $729 a month. This is roughly $102 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $98 (11%) lower.
