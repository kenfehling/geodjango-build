In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chickamauga is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Chickamauga is $138,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $18,140 (11%) lower.It is approximately $79,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $44,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median rent in Chickamauga is $729 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $102 (12%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $333 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $98 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
