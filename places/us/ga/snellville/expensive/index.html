In comparison to the surrounding areas, Snellville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Snellville is $169,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $27,580 (13%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $47,900 (22%) lower, and is approximately $120,800 (41%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Snellville is $1,469 per month. This is roughly $316 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $407 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $316 (27%) higher.
