In comparison to other places in this area, Snellville is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Snellville is valued at $169,600. This is approximately $27,580 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $47,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $120,800 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Snellville the median rent is $1,469 per month. It is about $316 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $407 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $316 (27%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
