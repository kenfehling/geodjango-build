Home prices

The median home in Americus costs $86,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $13,400 (18%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $131,300 (60%) lower, and is about $55,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Americus the median monthly rent is $744. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $25 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $162 (17%) lower.
