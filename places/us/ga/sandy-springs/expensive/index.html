In comparison to other places in this area, Sandy Springs is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Sandy Springs costs $471,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $127,240 (36%) higher.It is roughly $254,300 (116%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $181,400 (62%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Sandy Springs the median rent is $1,327 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21 (1%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $265 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $174 (15%) higher.
