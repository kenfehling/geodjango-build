In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sandy Springs is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandy Springs costs $471,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $127,240 (36%) higher. It is approximately $254,300 (116%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $181,400 (62%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sandy Springs is $1,327. It is approximately $21 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $265 (24%) higher, and is roughly $174 (15%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
