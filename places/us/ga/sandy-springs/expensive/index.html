Compared to nearby areas, Sandy Springs is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home value in Sandy Springs is $471,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $127,240 (36%) higher.It is roughly $254,300 (116%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $181,400 (62%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sandy Springs is $1,327 per month. This is about $21 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $265 (24%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $174 (15%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
