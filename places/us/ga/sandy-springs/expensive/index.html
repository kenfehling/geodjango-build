In comparison to nearby areas, Sandy Springs is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandy Springs costs $471,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $127,240 (36%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $254,300 (116%) higher, and it is roughly $181,400 (62%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Sandy Springs the median monthly rent is $1,327. It is about $21 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $265 (24%) higher, and is approximately $174 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
