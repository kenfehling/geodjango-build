Compared to the surrounding areas, Sandy Springs is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Atlanta, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandy Springs costs $471,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $127,240 (36%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $254,300 (116%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $181,400 (62%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sandy Springs is $1,327 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $21 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $265 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $174 (15%) higher.
