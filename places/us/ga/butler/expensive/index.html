Home prices

The cost of the median home in Butler is $73,900. This is about $5,820 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $143,600 (66%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $216,500 (74%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Butler the median rent is $446 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $11 (2%) lower.It is approximately $616 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $707 (61%) lower.
