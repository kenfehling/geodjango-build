Home prices

The median home in Barnesville costs $151,800. It is roughly $45,280 (42%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $65,700 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $138,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Barnesville is $654 a month. This is roughly $163 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $408 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $499 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
