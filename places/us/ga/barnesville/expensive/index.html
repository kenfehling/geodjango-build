Home prices

The median home cost in Barnesville is $151,800. This is approximately $45,280 (42%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $65,700 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $138,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

Barnesville's median monthly rent is $654. It is about $163 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $408 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $499 (43%) lower.
