Home prices

Barnesville's median home price is $151,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $45,280 (42%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $65,700 (30%) lower, and is roughly $138,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Barnesville's median rent is $654 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $163 (19%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $408 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $499 (43%) lower.
