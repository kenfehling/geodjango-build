Home prices

Chattanooga Valley's median home cost is $106,500. This is approximately $56,180 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $111,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $76,400 (41%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Chattanooga Valley's median rent is $788 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $31 (3%) lower. It is about $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $39 (4%) lower.
