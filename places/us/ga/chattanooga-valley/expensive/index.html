Home prices

The median home cost in Chattanooga Valley is $106,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $56,180 (34%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $111,000 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $76,400 (41%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Chattanooga Valley the median rent is $788 a month. This is about $31 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $274 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $39 (4%) lower.
