Home prices

The median home in Fairburn is valued at $140,600. This is roughly $46,920 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $76,900 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $149,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Fairburn the median monthly rent is $1,153. It is about $127 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $91 (8%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
