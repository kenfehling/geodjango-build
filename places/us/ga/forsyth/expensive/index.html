Home prices

Forsyth's median home value is $140,700. It is roughly $33,800 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $76,800 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $149,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Forsyth's median monthly rent is $692. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $15 (2%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $370 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $461 (39%) lower.
