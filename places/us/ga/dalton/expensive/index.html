The price of the median home in Dalton is $146,300. This is roughly $12,020 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $71,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $144,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Dalton's median rent is $699 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $16 (2%) lower. It is roughly $363 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $454 (39%) lower.
