Home prices

Acworth's median home cost is $182,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $31,680 (14%) lower. It is about $34,600 (15%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $107,500 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Acworth is $1,268. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $177 (16%) higher. It is approximately $206 (19%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $115 (9%) higher.
