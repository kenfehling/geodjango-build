Compared to its surrounding areas, Acworth is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Acworth costs $182,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $31,680 (14%) lower. It is roughly $34,600 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $107,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Acworth is $1,268. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $177 (16%) higher. It is approximately $206 (19%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $115 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
