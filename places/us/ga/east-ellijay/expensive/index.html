Home prices

The median home in East Ellijay costs $133,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $16,480 (11%) lower. It is about $84,200 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $157,100 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

East Ellijay's median monthly rent is $1,014. This is roughly $306 (43%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $48 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $139 (12%) lower.
