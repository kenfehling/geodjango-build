Compared to other places in this area, Jonesboro is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Jonesboro costs $80,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $21,900 (21%) lower. It is about $137,100 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $210,000 (72%) lower.

Rents

Jonesboro's median rent is $895 per month. It is approximately $151 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $167 (15%) lower, and is roughly $258 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
