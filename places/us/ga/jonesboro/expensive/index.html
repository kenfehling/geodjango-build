Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jonesboro is $80,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $21,900 (21%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $137,100 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $210,000 (72%) lower.

Rents

In Jonesboro the median rent is $895 per month. It is approximately $151 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $167 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $258 (22%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
