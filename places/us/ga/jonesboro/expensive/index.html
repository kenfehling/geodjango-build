Home prices

The median home value in Jonesboro is $80,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $21,900 (21%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $137,100 (63%) lower, and is approximately $210,000 (72%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Jonesboro's median monthly rent is $895. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $151 (14%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $167 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $258 (22%) lower.
