Home prices

The median home in Franklin Springs costs $127,700. This is about $45,620 (55%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $89,800 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $162,700 (56%) lower.

Rents

Franklin Springs's median rent is $702 a month. This is roughly $79 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $360 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $451 (39%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
