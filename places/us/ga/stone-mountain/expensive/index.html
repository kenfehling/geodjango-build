The median home price in Stone Mountain is $125,000. It is about $47,660 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $92,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $165,400 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Stone Mountain's median monthly rent is $1,104. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $24 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $42 (3%) higher, and is about $49 (4%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
