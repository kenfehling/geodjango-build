Compared to other places in this area, Chattahoochee Hills is a lot more expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chattahoochee Hills is valued at $226,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $95,380 (72%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $9,300 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $63,600 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Chattahoochee Hills the median rent is $969 per month. This is roughly $69 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $184 (15%) lower.
