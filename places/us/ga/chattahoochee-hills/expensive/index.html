Home prices

The price of the median home in Chattahoochee Hills is $226,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $95,380 (72%) higher. It is approximately $9,300 (4%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $63,600 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Chattahoochee Hills's median rent is $969 per month. It is roughly $69 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $93 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $184 (15%) lower.
