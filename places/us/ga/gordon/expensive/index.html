Home prices

The median home cost in Gordon is $67,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $7,140 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $150,300 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County it is roughly $53,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gordon is $649. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $103 (13%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $413 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County is roughly $169 (20%) lower.
