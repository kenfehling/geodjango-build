Rents

The median monthly rent in Richmond Hill is $1,529. It is about $520 (51%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $467 (43%) higher, and it is approximately $510 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.
