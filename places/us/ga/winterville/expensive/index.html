Compared to nearby areas, Winterville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winterville is valued at $154,800. This is approximately $8,600 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $62,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $135,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Winterville is $1,014 a month. This is about $305 (42%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $48 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $139 (12%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
