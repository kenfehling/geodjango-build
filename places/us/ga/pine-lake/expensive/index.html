Home prices

Pine Lake's median home cost is $172,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $42,400 (19%) lower. It is roughly $45,400 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $118,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Pine Lake the median rent is $1,042 per month. This is about $75 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $20 (1%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $111 (9%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
