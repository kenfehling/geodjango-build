Home prices

Pine Lake's median home value is $172,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $42,400 (19%) lower.It is roughly $45,400 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $118,300 (40%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Pine Lake is $1,042 per month. This is roughly $75 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $20 (1%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $111 (9%) lower.
