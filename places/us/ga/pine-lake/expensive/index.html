Compared to other places in this area, Pine Lake is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Pine Lake costs $172,100. It is approximately $42,400 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $45,400 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $118,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Pine Lake's median rent is $1,042 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $75 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $20 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $111 (9%) lower.
