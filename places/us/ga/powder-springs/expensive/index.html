Compared to the surrounding areas, Powder Springs is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Powder Springs costs $167,500. This is approximately $22,080 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $50,000 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $122,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Powder Springs the median monthly rent is $1,244. This is about $165 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $182 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $91 (7%) higher.
