Home prices

The median home price in Vinings is $481,000. This is about $171,200 (55%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $263,500 (121%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $190,600 (65%) higher.

Rents

Vinings's median monthly rent is $1,439. This is about $220 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $377 (35%) higher, and is approximately $286 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
