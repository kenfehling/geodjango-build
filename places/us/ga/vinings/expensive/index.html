Home prices

The value of the median home in Vinings is $481,000. It is roughly $171,200 (55%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $263,500 (121%) higher, and is roughly $190,600 (65%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Vinings's median rent is $1,439 a month. It is approximately $220 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $377 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $286 (24%) higher.
