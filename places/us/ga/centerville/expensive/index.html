Compared to its surrounding areas, Centerville is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Centerville is $144,300. This is approximately $17,820 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $73,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $3,000 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Centerville the median rent is $1,000 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $201 (25%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $62 (5%) lower, and is about $94 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
