Compared to other places in this area, Centerville is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

Centerville's median home value is $144,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $17,820 (14%) higher.It is about $73,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $3,000 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $1,000. It is approximately $201 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $62 (5%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $94 (10%) higher.
