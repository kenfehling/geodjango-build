Compared to its nearby areas, Centerville is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Centerville is $144,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $17,820 (14%) higher.It is roughly $73,200 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $3,000 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Centerville the median rent is $1,000 per month. This is about $201 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $62 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $94 (10%) higher.
