Compared to nearby areas, Centerville is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Centerville is valued at $144,300. This is about $17,820 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $73,200 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $3,000 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $1,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $201 (25%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $62 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $94 (10%) higher.
