Compared to its surrounding areas, Jefferson is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Jefferson's median home cost is $201,300. It is approximately $41,400 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $16,200 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $89,100 (30%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Jefferson's median rent is $729 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $137 (15%) lower. It is roughly $333 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $424 (36%) lower.
