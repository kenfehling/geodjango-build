Compared to other places in this area, Jefferson is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson costs $201,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $41,400 (25%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $16,200 (7%) lower, and it is roughly $89,100 (30%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Jefferson's median monthly rent is $729. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $137 (15%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $424 (36%) lower.
