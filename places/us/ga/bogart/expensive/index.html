Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bogart is $861. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $89 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $201 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $292 (25%) lower.
