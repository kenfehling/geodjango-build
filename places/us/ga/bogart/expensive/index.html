In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bogart is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Bogart's median home cost is $171,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $33,340 (24%) higher. It is about $46,200 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $119,100 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Bogart the median rent is $861 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $89 (9%) lower. It is roughly $201 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $292 (25%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
