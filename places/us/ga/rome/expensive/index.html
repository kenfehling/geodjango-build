Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rome is $151,200. It is roughly $57,600 (61%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $66,300 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $139,200 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Rome the median monthly rent is $724. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $35 (4%) lower. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $429 (37%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
