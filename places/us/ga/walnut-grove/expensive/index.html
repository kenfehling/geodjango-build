Compared to its surrounding areas, Walnut Grove is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Walnut Grove's median home cost is $161,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $16,360 (11%) higher. It is about $55,600 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $128,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Walnut Grove is $1,115 per month. This is about $114 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $53 (4%) higher, and is roughly $38 (3%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
