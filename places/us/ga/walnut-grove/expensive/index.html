Home prices

The median home in Walnut Grove costs $161,900. It is about $16,360 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $55,600 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $128,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Walnut Grove is $1,115 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $114 (11%) higher. It is approximately $53 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $38 (3%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
