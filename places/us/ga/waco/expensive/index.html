Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waco is $105,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $22,020 (17%) lower. It is roughly $111,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $184,600 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Waco the median monthly rent is $771. This is about $62 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $291 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $382 (33%) lower.
