In comparison to its nearby areas, Waco is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waco costs $105,800. This is roughly $22,020 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $111,700 (51%) lower, and it is about $184,600 (63%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Waco's median monthly rent is $771. This is about $62 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $291 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $382 (33%) lower.
