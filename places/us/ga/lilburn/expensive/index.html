In comparison to other places in this area, Lilburn is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lilburn is valued at $175,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $60,340 (25%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $42,200 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $115,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

Lilburn's median monthly rent is $1,089. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $188 (14%) lower.It is about $27 (2%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $64 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
