Compared to its surrounding areas, Lilburn is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lilburn is $175,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $60,340 (25%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $42,200 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $115,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Lilburn the median monthly rent is $1,089. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $188 (14%) lower. It is about $27 (2%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $64 (5%) lower.
