Home prices

The median home in Holly Springs costs $268,900. It is approximately $20,120 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $51,400 (23%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $21,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Holly Springs the median monthly rent is $1,288. This is roughly $43 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $226 (21%) higher, and is approximately $135 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
