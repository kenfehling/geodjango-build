Home prices

The median home in Ellijay is valued at $173,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,560 (12%) higher. It is approximately $44,500 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $117,400 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Ellijay is $614 a month. This is roughly $156 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $448 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $539 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
