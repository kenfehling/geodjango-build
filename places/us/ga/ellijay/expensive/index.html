Compared to the surrounding areas, Ellijay is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ellijay is valued at $173,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $19,560 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $44,500 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $117,400 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Ellijay the median rent is $614 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $156 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $448 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $539 (46%) lower.
