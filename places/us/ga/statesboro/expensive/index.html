Home prices

The median home price in Statesboro is $113,600. It is approximately $7,060 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is roughly $42,900 (27%) lower.

Rents

Statesboro's median monthly rent is $780. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $8 (1%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is about $239 (23%) lower.
