Home prices

Statesboro's median home value is $113,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $7,060 (6%) higher.It is roughly $103,900 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $42,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Statesboro is $780 a month. It is roughly $8 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $282 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $239 (23%) lower.
