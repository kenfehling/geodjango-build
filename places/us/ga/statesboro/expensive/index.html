In comparison to the surrounding areas, Statesboro is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Savannah, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Statesboro is valued at $113,600. It is approximately $7,060 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is roughly $42,900 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Statesboro is $780 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $8 (1%) higher. It is about $282 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is approximately $239 (23%) lower.
