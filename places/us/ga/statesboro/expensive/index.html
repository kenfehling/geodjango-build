Home prices

The price of the median home in Statesboro is $113,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $7,060 (6%) higher.It is roughly $103,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $42,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Statesboro the median rent is $780 a month. This is approximately $8 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $282 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is roughly $239 (23%) lower.
