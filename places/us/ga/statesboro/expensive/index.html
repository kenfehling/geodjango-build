Compared to nearby areas, Statesboro is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home in Statesboro costs $113,600. This is roughly $7,060 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $103,900 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $42,900 (27%) lower.

Rents

Statesboro's median rent is $780 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $8 (1%) higher. It is about $282 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is roughly $239 (23%) lower.
