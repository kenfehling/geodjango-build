Home prices

The median home in Tyrone costs $286,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $93,480 (48%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $69,000 (31%) higher, and is about $3,900 (1%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Tyrone the median monthly rent is $999. This is about $142 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $63 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $154 (13%) lower.
