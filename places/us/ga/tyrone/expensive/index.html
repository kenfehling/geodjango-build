Home prices

The median home price in Tyrone is $286,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $93,480 (48%) higher. It is roughly $69,000 (31%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $3,900 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tyrone is $999. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $142 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $63 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $154 (13%) lower.
