Home prices

The median home in Gibson costs $98,900. This is roughly $29,340 (42%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $118,600 (54%) lower, and it is approximately $21,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Rents

The median rent in Gibson is $611 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $128 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $451 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County is roughly $207 (25%) lower.
