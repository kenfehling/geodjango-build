Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fair Oaks is $126,000. This is roughly $154,580 (55%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $91,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $164,400 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fair Oaks is $940. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $300 (24%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $122 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $213 (18%) lower.
