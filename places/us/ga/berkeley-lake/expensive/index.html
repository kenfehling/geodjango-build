Home prices

The median home in Berkeley Lake is valued at $442,000. It is approximately $176,140 (66%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $224,500 (103%) higher, and is approximately $151,600 (52%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Berkeley Lake's median rent is $1,821 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $500 (37%) higher. It is approximately $759 (71%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $668 (57%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
