Compared to nearby areas, Duluth is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Duluth is $229,600. It is approximately $105,000 (31%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $12,100 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $60,800 (20%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Duluth is $1,366 per month. This is roughly $108 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $304 (28%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $213 (18%) higher.
