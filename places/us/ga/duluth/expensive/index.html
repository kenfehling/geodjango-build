Home prices

The median home value in Duluth is $229,600. This is about $105,000 (31%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $12,100 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $60,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Duluth is $1,366. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $108 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $304 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $213 (18%) higher.
