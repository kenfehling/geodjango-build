Compared to other places in this area, Duluth is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Duluth is $229,600. This is about $105,000 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $12,100 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $60,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

Duluth's median monthly rent is $1,366. This is roughly $108 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $304 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $213 (18%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
