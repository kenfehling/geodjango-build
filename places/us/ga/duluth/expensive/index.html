Home prices

The median home cost in Duluth is $229,600. It is roughly $105,000 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $12,100 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $60,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

Duluth's median monthly rent is $1,366. Compared to other places in this area it is about $108 (7%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $304 (28%) higher, and it is approximately $213 (18%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
