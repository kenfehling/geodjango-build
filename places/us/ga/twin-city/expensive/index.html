Home prices

The median home in Twin City is valued at $74,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $6,020 (7%) lower. It is about $142,900 (65%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $81,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Twin City the median rent is $555 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $105 (15%) lower. It is roughly $507 (47%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $464 (45%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
