Home prices

The median home in Twin City costs $74,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $6,020 (7%) lower.It is roughly $142,900 (65%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $81,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Twin City the median rent is $555 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $105 (15%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $507 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is roughly $464 (45%) lower.
