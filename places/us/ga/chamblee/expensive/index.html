Compared to the surrounding areas, Chamblee is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home cost in Chamblee is $283,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $103,400 (26%) lower. It is roughly $65,800 (30%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chamblee is $1,292. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $77 (5%) lower. It is roughly $230 (21%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $139 (12%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
