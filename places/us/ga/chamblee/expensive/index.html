Home prices

Chamblee's median home price is $283,300. It is approximately $103,400 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $65,800 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

Chamblee's median monthly rent is $1,292. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $77 (5%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $230 (21%) higher, and is about $139 (12%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
