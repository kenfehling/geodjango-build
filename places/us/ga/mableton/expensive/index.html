Home prices

The median home in Mableton costs $182,800. It is approximately $56,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $34,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $107,600 (37%) lower.

Rents

Mableton's median rent is $1,166 a month. This is about $46 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $104 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $13 (1%) higher.
