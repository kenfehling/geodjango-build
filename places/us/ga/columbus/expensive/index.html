In comparison to the surrounding areas, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Montgomery.

Home prices

The median home cost in Columbus is $141,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $26,800 (23%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $76,200 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Montgomery it is roughly $20,100 (16%) higher.

Rents

Columbus's median monthly rent is $906. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $161 (21%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $156 (14%) lower, and is about $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Montgomery.
