Compared to its nearby areas, Columbus is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Montgomery, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Columbus is $141,300. It is roughly $26,800 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $76,200 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Montgomery it is about $20,100 (16%) higher.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $906 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $161 (21%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $156 (14%) lower, and is roughly $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Montgomery.
