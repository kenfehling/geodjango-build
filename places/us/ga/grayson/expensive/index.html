Home prices

The median home in Grayson costs $311,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $133,000 (74%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $94,200 (43%) higher, and is approximately $21,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Grayson the median monthly rent is $1,368. This is about $150 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $306 (28%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $215 (18%) higher.
