Home prices

The cost of the median home in Grayson is $311,700. It is approximately $133,000 (74%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $94,200 (43%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $21,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Grayson the median rent is $1,368 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $150 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $306 (28%) higher, and it is approximately $215 (18%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
