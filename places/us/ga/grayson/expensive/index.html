Home prices

The median home in Grayson is valued at $311,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $133,000 (74%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $94,200 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $21,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Grayson the median rent is $1,368 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $150 (12%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $306 (28%) higher, and is approximately $215 (18%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
