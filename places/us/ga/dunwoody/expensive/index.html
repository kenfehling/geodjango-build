Home prices

The median home in Dunwoody costs $432,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $80,560 (22%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $215,400 (99%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $142,500 (49%) higher.

Rents

In Dunwoody the median monthly rent is $1,468. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $190 (14%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $406 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $315 (27%) higher.
