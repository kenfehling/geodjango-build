In comparison to other places in this area, Dunwoody is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dunwoody is $432,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $80,560 (22%) higher.It is roughly $215,400 (99%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $142,500 (49%) higher.

Rents

Dunwoody's median rent is $1,468 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $190 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $406 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $315 (27%) higher.
