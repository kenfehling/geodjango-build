Home prices

The value of the median home in Cochran is $127,600. It is roughly $40,840 (47%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $89,900 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County it is roughly $7,400 (6%) higher.

Rents

Cochran's median rent is $615 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $10 (1%) higher. It is approximately $447 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $203 (24%) lower compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
