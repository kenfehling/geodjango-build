In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rochelle is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Rochelle is $86,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $28,080 (48%) higher. It is about $131,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $34,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Rents

In Rochelle the median rent is $731 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $241 (49%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $331 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $87 (10%) lower compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
