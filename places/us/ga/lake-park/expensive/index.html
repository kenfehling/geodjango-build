Home prices

The median home in Lake Park is valued at $180,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $92,520 (105%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $37,000 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $7,300 (4%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Lake Park's median monthly rent is $804. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $152 (23%) higher.It is roughly $258 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $261 (24%) lower.
