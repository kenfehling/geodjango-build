Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Park is $180,500. This is approximately $92,520 (105%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $37,000 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $7,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Park the median rent is $804 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $152 (23%) higher. It is approximately $258 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $261 (24%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
