Rents

The median rent in Lake Park is $804 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $152 (23%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $258 (24%) lower, and is roughly $261 (24%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
