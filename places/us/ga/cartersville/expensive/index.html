Home prices

The median home in Cartersville costs $188,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $54,460 (40%) higher.It is roughly $28,600 (13%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $101,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Cartersville the median monthly rent is $886. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $46 (4%) lower.It is roughly $176 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $267 (23%) lower.
