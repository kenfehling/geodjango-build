Home prices

The median home cost in Cartersville is $188,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $54,460 (40%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $28,600 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $101,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Cartersville the median rent is $886 per month. It is approximately $46 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $176 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $267 (23%) lower.
