Home prices

Rents

Nashville's median rent is $590 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (11%) lower.It is approximately $472 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tallahassee is about $433 (42%) lower.
