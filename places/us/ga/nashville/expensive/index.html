Home prices

The median home in Nashville costs $81,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,080 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $136,200 (62%) lower, and it is roughly $122,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median rent in Nashville is $590 a month. This is roughly $78 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $472 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Tallahassee is about $433 (42%) lower.
