Home prices

The value of the median home in Nashville is $81,300. It is approximately $3,080 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $136,200 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $122,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nashville is $590. Compared to nearby areas it is about $78 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $472 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee it is about $433 (42%) lower.
