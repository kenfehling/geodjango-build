Home prices

The median home price in Nashville is $81,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $3,080 (3%) higher.It is about $136,200 (62%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $122,500 (60%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Nashville the median monthly rent is $590. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $78 (11%) lower.It is roughly $472 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $433 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
