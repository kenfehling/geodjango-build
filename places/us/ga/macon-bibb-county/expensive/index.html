Home prices

Macon-Bibb County's median home cost is $120,200. It is roughly $12,360 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $97,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $170,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Macon-Bibb County is $818 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14 (1%) lower.It is approximately $244 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $335 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
