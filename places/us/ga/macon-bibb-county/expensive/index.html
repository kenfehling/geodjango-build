Home prices

The median home in Macon-Bibb County is valued at $120,200. This is approximately $12,360 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $97,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $170,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Macon-Bibb County the median rent is $818 per month. This is about $14 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $244 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $335 (29%) lower.
