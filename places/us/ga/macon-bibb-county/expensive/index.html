Home prices

The median home in Macon-Bibb County costs $120,200. This is roughly $12,360 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $97,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $170,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

Macon-Bibb County's median monthly rent is $818. Compared to other places in this area it is about $14 (1%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $244 (22%) lower, and is approximately $335 (29%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
