Home prices

The median home in St. Marys costs $182,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $3,780 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $35,100 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $9,200 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in St. Marys is $1,025 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $72 (7%) higher. It is approximately $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is about $40 (3%) lower.
