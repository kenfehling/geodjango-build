Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Marys is $182,400. It is roughly $3,780 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $35,100 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $9,200 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

St. Marys's median monthly rent is $1,025. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $72 (7%) higher.It is approximately $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $40 (3%) lower.
