Home prices

The median home in St. Marys is valued at $182,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $3,780 (2%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $35,100 (16%) lower, and is roughly $9,200 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In St. Marys the median monthly rent is $1,025. It is approximately $72 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $37 (3%) lower, and is roughly $40 (3%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
