Home prices

The median home in St. Marys costs $182,400. It is roughly $3,780 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $35,100 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $9,200 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Marys is $1,025. It is about $72 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $40 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
