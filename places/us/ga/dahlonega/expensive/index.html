Rents

Dahlonega's median monthly rent is $990. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $152 (18%) higher.It is about $72 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $163 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
