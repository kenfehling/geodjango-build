Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dahlonega is $285,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $98,600 (52%) higher. It is roughly $68,200 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $4,700 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Dahlonega's median rent is $990 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $152 (18%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $72 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $163 (14%) lower.
