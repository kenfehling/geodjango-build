Home prices

Rents

In Hazlehurst the median rent is $610 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $58 (10%) higher.It is approximately $452 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is about $409 (40%) lower.
