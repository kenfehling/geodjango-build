Rents

The median rent in Hazlehurst is $610 per month. This is approximately $58 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $452 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $409 (40%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
