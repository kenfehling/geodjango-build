Compared to its nearby areas, Dublin is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dublin is $102,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $12,440 (13%) higher. It is about $115,100 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $17,800 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Rents

Dublin's median monthly rent is $589. This is about $113 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $473 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County is approximately $229 (27%) lower.
