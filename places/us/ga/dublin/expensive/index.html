Compared to other places in this area, Dublin is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Dublin's median home value is $102,400. It is approximately $12,440 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $115,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $17,800 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Rents

Dublin's median monthly rent is $589. This is roughly $113 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $473 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $229 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
