Compared to other places in this area, Dublin is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Home prices

The median home in Dublin is valued at $102,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12,440 (13%) higher. It is about $115,100 (52%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $17,800 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.

Rents

The median rent in Dublin is $589 a month. It is roughly $113 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $473 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County is about $229 (27%) lower.
