In comparison to other places in this area, Dublin is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dublin is valued at $102,400. It is approximately $12,440 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $115,100 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County is roughly $17,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Dublin the median rent is $589 a month. This is about $113 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $473 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County is roughly $229 (27%) lower.
