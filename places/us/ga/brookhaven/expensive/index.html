Home prices

The median home in Brookhaven is valued at $484,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $137,920 (39%) higher. It is approximately $266,900 (122%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $194,000 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookhaven is $1,415. This is about $71 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $353 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $262 (22%) higher.
