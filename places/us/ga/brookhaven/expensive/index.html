Home prices

The median home in Brookhaven is valued at $484,400. This is about $137,920 (39%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $266,900 (122%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $194,000 (66%) higher.

Rents

Brookhaven's median monthly rent is $1,415. It is about $71 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $353 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $262 (22%) higher.
