Home prices

The median home in Brookhaven is valued at $484,400. It is roughly $137,920 (39%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $266,900 (122%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $194,000 (66%) higher.

Rents

Brookhaven's median rent is $1,415 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $71 (5%) higher. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $262 (22%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
