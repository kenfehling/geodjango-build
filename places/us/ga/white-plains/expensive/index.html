Home prices

The cost of the median home in White Plains is $73,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $16,060 (28%) higher.It is roughly $144,200 (66%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $217,100 (74%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in White Plains is $492. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $161 (24%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $570 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $661 (57%) lower.
