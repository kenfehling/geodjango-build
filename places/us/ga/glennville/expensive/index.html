Home prices

The median home price in Glennville is $122,000. This is approximately $8,820 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $95,500 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $34,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Glennville the median rent is $597 per month. This is roughly $27 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $465 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $422 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
