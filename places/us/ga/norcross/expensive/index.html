Home prices

Norcross's median home price is $177,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $137,080 (43%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $40,100 (18%) lower, and is approximately $113,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Norcross is $1,205. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $142 (10%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $143 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $52 (4%) higher.
