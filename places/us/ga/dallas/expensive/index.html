Compared to the surrounding areas, Dallas is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dallas costs $154,300. This is about $20,400 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $63,200 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $136,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Dallas's median rent is $1,042 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $36 (3%) higher.It is approximately $20 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $111 (9%) lower.
