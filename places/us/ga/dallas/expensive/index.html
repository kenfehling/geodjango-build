Compared to the surrounding areas, Dallas is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dallas is $154,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $20,400 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $63,200 (29%) lower, and it is about $136,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Dallas's median rent is $1,042 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $36 (3%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $20 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $111 (9%) lower.
