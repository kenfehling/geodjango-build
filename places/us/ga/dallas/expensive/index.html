In comparison to nearby areas, Dallas is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home price in Dallas is $154,300. It is roughly $20,400 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $63,200 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $136,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Dallas's median monthly rent is $1,042. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $36 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $20 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $111 (9%) lower.
