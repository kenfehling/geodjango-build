Compared to the surrounding areas, Dallas is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Dallas is $154,300. This is roughly $20,400 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $63,200 (29%) lower, and is approximately $136,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Dallas the median rent is $1,042 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $36 (3%) higher. It is about $20 (1%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $111 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
