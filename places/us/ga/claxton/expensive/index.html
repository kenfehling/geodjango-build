Home prices

The median home cost in Claxton is $89,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $17,240 (16%) lower. It is about $128,300 (58%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $67,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Claxton the median rent is $581 a month. This is about $22 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $481 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $438 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
