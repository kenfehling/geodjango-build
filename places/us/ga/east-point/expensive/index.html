Home prices

The median home in East Point costs $139,800. It is approximately $22,080 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $77,700 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $150,600 (51%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In East Point the median rent is $1,035 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $58 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $27 (2%) lower, and it is approximately $118 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
