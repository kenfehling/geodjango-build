Compared to the surrounding areas, East Point is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

East Point's median home price is $139,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $22,080 (18%) higher. It is roughly $77,700 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $150,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in East Point is $1,035 per month. It is roughly $58 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $27 (2%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $118 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
