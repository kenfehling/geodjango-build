Home prices

The price of the median home in East Point is $139,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $22,080 (18%) higher.It is about $77,700 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $150,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In East Point the median rent is $1,035 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $58 (5%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $27 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $118 (10%) lower.
