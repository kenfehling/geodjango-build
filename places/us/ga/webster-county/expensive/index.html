The median home in Webster County costs $65,700. This is approximately $1,580 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $151,800 (69%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $75,600 (53%) lower.

Rents

Webster County's median rent is $381 a month. It is approximately $205 (34%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $681 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $525 (57%) lower.
