In comparison to its surrounding areas, Druid Hills is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Atlanta, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Druid Hills's median home price is $645,600. It is approximately $295,020 (84%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $428,100 (196%) higher, and is approximately $355,200 (122%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Druid Hills the median monthly rent is $1,475. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $263 (21%) higher. It is about $413 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $322 (27%) higher.
