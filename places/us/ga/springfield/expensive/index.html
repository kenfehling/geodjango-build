Home prices

The value of the median home in Springfield is $115,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $58,600 (33%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $101,700 (46%) lower, and is about $40,700 (26%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Springfield the median rent is $964 a month. It is about $109 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $98 (9%) lower, and it is about $55 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
