Compared to the surrounding areas, Union City is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Union City costs $132,300. It is roughly $18,920 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $85,200 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $158,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Union City is $967 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $87 (8%) lower. It is approximately $95 (8%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $186 (16%) lower.
