Compared to its surrounding areas, Union City is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Union City is valued at $132,300. This is approximately $18,920 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $85,200 (39%) lower, and is roughly $158,100 (54%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Union City is $967. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $87 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $95 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $186 (16%) lower.
