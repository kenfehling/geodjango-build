Home prices

Lake City's median home cost is $88,200. It is approximately $2,900 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $129,300 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $202,200 (69%) lower.

Rents

Lake City's median rent is $939 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $17 (1%) higher.It is about $123 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $214 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
