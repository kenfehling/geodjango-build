Home prices

The value of the median home in Lake City is $88,200. It is roughly $2,900 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $129,300 (59%) lower, and it is roughly $202,200 (69%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Lake City the median rent is $939 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $17 (1%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $123 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $214 (18%) lower.
