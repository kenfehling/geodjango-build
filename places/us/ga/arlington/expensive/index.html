Home prices

The median home cost in Arlington is $43,000. It is about $18,580 (30%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $174,500 (80%) lower, and it is approximately $160,800 (78%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median rent in Arlington is $447 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $121 (21%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $615 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $576 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
