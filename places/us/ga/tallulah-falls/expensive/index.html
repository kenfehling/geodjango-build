Home prices

Rents

Tallulah Falls's median monthly rent is $569. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $102 (15%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $493 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Sandy Springs is roughly $758 (57%) lower.
