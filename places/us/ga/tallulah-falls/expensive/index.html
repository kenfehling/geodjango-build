Home prices

The median home in Tallulah Falls costs $170,800. This is about $50,640 (42%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $46,700 (21%) lower, and is approximately $301,000 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Sandy Springs.

Rents

The median rent in Tallulah Falls is $569 per month. This is roughly $102 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $493 (46%) lower, and is about $758 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Sandy Springs.
