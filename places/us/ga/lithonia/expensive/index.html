Compared to other places in this area, Lithonia is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lithonia is $89,600. It is roughly $16,780 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $127,900 (58%) lower, and is about $200,800 (69%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Lithonia's median rent is $917 per month. This is about $87 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $145 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $236 (20%) lower.
