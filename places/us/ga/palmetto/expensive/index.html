In comparison to nearby areas, Palmetto is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palmetto is valued at $129,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $60,600 (31%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $88,300 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $161,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Palmetto the median rent is $1,018 a month. This is roughly $35 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $44 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $135 (11%) lower.
