Compared to the surrounding areas, Palmetto is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Palmetto is $129,200. This is approximately $60,600 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $88,300 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $161,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Palmetto the median rent is $1,018 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $35 (3%) lower.It is approximately $44 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $135 (11%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
