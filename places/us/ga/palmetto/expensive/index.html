In comparison to other places in this area, Palmetto is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home price in Palmetto is $129,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $60,600 (31%) lower.It is approximately $88,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $161,200 (55%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Palmetto's median rent is $1,018 per month. This is approximately $35 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $44 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $135 (11%) lower.
