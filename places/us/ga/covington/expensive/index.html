Compared to the surrounding areas, Covington is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

Covington's median home cost is $171,600. This is about $53,520 (45%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $45,900 (21%) lower, and is about $118,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Covington is $874 a month. It is approximately $45 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $188 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $279 (24%) lower.
