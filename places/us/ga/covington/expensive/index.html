Compared to its surrounding areas, Covington is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Covington is $171,600. This is roughly $53,520 (45%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $45,900 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $118,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Covington is $874. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $45 (4%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $188 (17%) lower, and is roughly $279 (24%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
