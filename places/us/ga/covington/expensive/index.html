Compared to its surrounding areas, Covington is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Covington costs $171,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $53,520 (45%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $45,900 (21%) lower, and is approximately $118,800 (40%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Covington the median monthly rent is $874. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $45 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $188 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $279 (24%) lower.
