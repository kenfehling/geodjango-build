Home prices

The median home cost in Covington is $171,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $53,520 (45%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $45,900 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $118,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Covington is $874. Compared to nearby areas this is about $45 (4%) lower.It is about $188 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $279 (24%) lower.
