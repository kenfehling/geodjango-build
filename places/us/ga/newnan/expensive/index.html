Home prices

The median home in Newnan costs $206,300. It is about $4,320 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $11,200 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $84,100 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Newnan the median monthly rent is $1,039. It is about $37 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $23 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $114 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
