Compared to its nearby areas, South Fulton is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Fulton costs $162,800. It is roughly $17,680 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $54,700 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $127,600 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Fulton is $1,176. This is about $164 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $114 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $23 (1%) higher.
