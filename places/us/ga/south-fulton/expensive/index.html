Home prices

The median home in South Fulton is valued at $162,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $17,680 (12%) higher. It is approximately $54,700 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $127,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in South Fulton is $1,176 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $164 (16%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $114 (10%) higher, and it is roughly $23 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
