Home prices

The median home in South Fulton is valued at $162,800. It is about $17,680 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $54,700 (25%) lower, and is about $127,600 (43%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In South Fulton the median rent is $1,176 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $164 (16%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $114 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $23 (1%) higher.
