Home prices

The value of the median home in Henderson is $181,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $7,480 (4%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $36,500 (16%) lower, and it is about $24,500 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Henderson is $1,101 a month. It is roughly $20 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $39 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is approximately $82 (8%) higher.
