Home prices

Henderson's median home value is $181,000. This is approximately $7,480 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $36,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $24,500 (15%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Henderson's median rent is $1,101 a month. It is roughly $20 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $39 (3%) higher, and is roughly $82 (8%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
