Home prices

The median home in Henderson costs $181,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $7,480 (4%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $36,500 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah is roughly $24,500 (15%) higher.

Rents

Henderson's median rent is $1,101 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $20 (1%) lower.It is about $39 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $82 (8%) higher.
