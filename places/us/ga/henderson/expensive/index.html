Home prices

The median home in Henderson is valued at $181,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $7,480 (4%) higher. It is about $36,500 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is approximately $24,500 (15%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Henderson is $1,101 per month. This is about $20 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $39 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $82 (8%) higher.
