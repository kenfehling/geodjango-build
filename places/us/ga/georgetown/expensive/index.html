In comparison to nearby areas, Georgetown is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Savannah, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown costs $178,800. This is about $39,560 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $38,700 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $22,300 (14%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Georgetown is $1,105. This is roughly $89 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $43 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is approximately $86 (8%) higher.
