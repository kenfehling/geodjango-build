In comparison to nearby areas, Georgetown is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown costs $178,800. This is about $39,560 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $38,700 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $22,300 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Georgetown the median monthly rent is $1,105. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $89 (7%) lower.It is about $43 (4%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $86 (8%) higher.
