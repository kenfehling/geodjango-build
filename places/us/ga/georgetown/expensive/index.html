Home prices

The cost of the median home in Georgetown is $178,800. This is about $39,560 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $38,700 (17%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $22,300 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Georgetown's median rent is $1,105 a month. This is about $89 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $43 (4%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $86 (8%) higher.
