Compared to its surrounding areas, Georgetown is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home cost in Georgetown is $178,800. It is about $39,560 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $38,700 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Savannah it is roughly $22,300 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Georgetown the median rent is $1,105 a month. It is about $89 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $43 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is roughly $86 (8%) higher.
