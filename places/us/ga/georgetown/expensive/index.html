In comparison to nearby areas, Georgetown is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Savannah, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown is valued at $178,800. It is approximately $39,560 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $38,700 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is about $22,300 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Georgetown is $1,105 per month. This is about $89 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $43 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $86 (8%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
