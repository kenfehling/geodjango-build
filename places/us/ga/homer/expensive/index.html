Home prices

The median home in Homer costs $170,000. This is about $42,240 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $47,500 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $120,400 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Homer is $692 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $144 (17%) lower. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $461 (39%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
