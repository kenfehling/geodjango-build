Compared to the surrounding areas, Homer is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Homer is valued at $170,000. It is about $42,240 (33%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $47,500 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $120,400 (41%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Homer is $692. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $144 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $461 (39%) lower.
