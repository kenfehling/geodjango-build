Home prices

The median home in Homer is valued at $170,000. It is roughly $42,240 (33%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $47,500 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $120,400 (41%) lower.

Rents

Homer's median monthly rent is $692. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $144 (17%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and it is about $461 (39%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
