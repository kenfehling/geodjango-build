Home prices

The value of the median home in Cornelia is $125,800. This is roughly $18,280 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $91,700 (42%) lower, and is about $164,600 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Cornelia is $785 per month. It is approximately $42 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $277 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $368 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
