In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lawrenceville is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Lawrenceville's median home price is $171,600. It is about $56,440 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $45,900 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $118,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

Lawrenceville's median monthly rent is $1,027. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $321 (23%) lower. It is approximately $35 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $126 (10%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
