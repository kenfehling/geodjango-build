Home prices

The median home value in Lawrenceville is $171,600. It is about $56,440 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $45,900 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $118,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lawrenceville is $1,027. This is about $321 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $35 (3%) lower, and it is about $126 (10%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
