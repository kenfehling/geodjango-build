Home prices

The median home value in Buford is $239,100. This is approximately $9,500 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $21,600 (9%) higher, and is approximately $51,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Buford's median monthly rent is $969. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $279 (22%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $93 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $184 (15%) lower.
