Home prices

The median home price in Buford is $239,100. This is approximately $9,500 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $21,600 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $51,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

Buford's median monthly rent is $969. This is roughly $279 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $93 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $184 (15%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
