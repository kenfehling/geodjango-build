Home prices

The price of the median home in Buford is $239,100. It is approximately $9,500 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $21,600 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $51,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

Buford's median monthly rent is $969. It is about $279 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $93 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $184 (15%) lower.
