Home prices

The median home in Whitemarsh Island is valued at $246,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $63,560 (20%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $28,900 (13%) higher, and is about $89,900 (57%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Whitemarsh Island is $1,257 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $20 (1%) higher. It is approximately $195 (18%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $238 (23%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
