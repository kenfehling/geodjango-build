Home prices

The median home in Whitemarsh Island is valued at $246,400. This is approximately $63,560 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $28,900 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $89,900 (57%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Whitemarsh Island's median rent is $1,257 a month. It is approximately $20 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $195 (18%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $238 (23%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
