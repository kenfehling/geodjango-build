Rents

The median monthly rent in Calhoun is $688. It is about $134 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $465 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
