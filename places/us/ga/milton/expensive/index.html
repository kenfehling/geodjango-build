In comparison to nearby areas, Milton is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milton costs $541,000. This is roughly $190,700 (54%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $323,500 (148%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $250,600 (86%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Milton's median rent is $1,359 per month. It is roughly $30 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $297 (27%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $206 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
