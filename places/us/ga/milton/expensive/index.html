Compared to the surrounding areas, Milton is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milton is valued at $541,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $190,700 (54%) higher.It is about $323,500 (148%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $250,600 (86%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Milton is $1,359 a month. It is roughly $30 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $297 (27%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $206 (17%) higher.
