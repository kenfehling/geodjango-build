In comparison to other places in this area, Milton is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Atlanta, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Milton's median home price is $541,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $190,700 (54%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $323,500 (148%) higher, and is approximately $250,600 (86%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Milton is $1,359 per month. This is about $30 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $297 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $206 (17%) higher.
