Compared to its surrounding areas, Milton is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

Milton's median home value is $541,000. This is approximately $190,700 (54%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $323,500 (148%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $250,600 (86%) higher.

Rents

Milton's median rent is $1,359 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $30 (2%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $297 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $206 (17%) higher.
