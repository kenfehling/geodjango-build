Home prices

The median home in Talmo is valued at $156,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $60,460 (27%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $61,200 (28%) lower, and is about $134,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Talmo is $867 a month. It is approximately $114 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $286 (24%) lower.
