Home prices

The median home in McDonough is valued at $164,100. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $4,540 (2%) higher.It is approximately $53,400 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $126,300 (43%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

McDonough's median rent is $1,137 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $17 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $75 (7%) higher, and it is approximately $16 (1%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
