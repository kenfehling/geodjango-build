Home prices

The median home in McDonough is valued at $164,100. It is approximately $4,540 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $53,400 (24%) lower, and it is about $126,300 (43%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In McDonough the median monthly rent is $1,137. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $17 (1%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $75 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $16 (1%) lower.
