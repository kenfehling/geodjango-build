Much like other places in this area, it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in McDonough costs $164,100. It is about $4,540 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $53,400 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $126,300 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in McDonough is $1,137 a month. This is about $17 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $75 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $16 (1%) lower.
