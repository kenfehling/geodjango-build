Home prices

The value of the median home in McDonough is $164,100. This is approximately $4,540 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $53,400 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $126,300 (43%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

McDonough's median monthly rent is $1,137. This is about $17 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $75 (7%) higher, and is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
