Compared to nearby areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

Cleveland's median home value is $159,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $38,940 (19%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $57,800 (26%) lower, and is roughly $130,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Cleveland's median monthly rent is $738. It is about $86 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $324 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $415 (35%) lower.
