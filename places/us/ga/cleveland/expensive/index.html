In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $159,700. This is approximately $38,940 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $57,800 (26%) lower, and is about $130,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $738 per month. It is approximately $86 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $324 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $415 (35%) lower.
