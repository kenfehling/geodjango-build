Home prices

The median home value in Doraville is $177,600. This is about $166,920 (48%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $112,800 (38%) lower.

Rents

Doraville's median monthly rent is $1,178. This is roughly $134 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $116 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
