Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Pine Mountain is $725 a month. This is roughly $51 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and it is about $428 (37%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
