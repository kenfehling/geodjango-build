Home prices

The median home cost in Dawsonville is $191,900. This is about $27,020 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $25,600 (11%) lower, and is roughly $98,500 (33%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dawsonville is $828. It is roughly $115 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $234 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $325 (28%) lower.
