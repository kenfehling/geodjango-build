Home prices

Dawsonville's median home value is $191,900. This is approximately $27,020 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $25,600 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $98,500 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Dawsonville the median monthly rent is $828. This is about $115 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $234 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $325 (28%) lower.
