Compared to other places in this area, Dawsonville is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dawsonville is $191,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $27,020 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $25,600 (11%) lower, and is about $98,500 (33%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Dawsonville the median monthly rent is $828. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $115 (12%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $234 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $325 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
