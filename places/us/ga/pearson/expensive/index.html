Home prices

The median home cost in Pearson is $61,700. This is roughly $3,020 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $155,800 (71%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $38,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

In Pearson the median rent is $510 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $39 (7%) lower. It is approximately $552 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Albany is approximately $228 (30%) lower.
