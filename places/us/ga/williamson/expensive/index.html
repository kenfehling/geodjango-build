Home prices

The median home in Williamson is valued at $160,500. This is about $65,920 (69%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $57,000 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $129,900 (44%) lower.

Rents

Williamson's median rent is $815 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $39 (5%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $247 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $338 (29%) lower.
