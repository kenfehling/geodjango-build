Home prices

The median home in Alpharetta is valued at $422,800. It is about $33,720 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $205,300 (94%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $132,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alpharetta is $1,451. Compared to nearby areas this is about $102 (7%) higher.It is about $389 (36%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is roughly $298 (25%) higher.
