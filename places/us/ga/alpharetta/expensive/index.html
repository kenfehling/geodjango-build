In comparison to the surrounding areas, Alpharetta is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alpharetta is $422,800. This is roughly $33,720 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $205,300 (94%) higher, and is approximately $132,400 (45%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Alpharetta is $1,451 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $102 (7%) higher. It is about $389 (36%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $298 (25%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
