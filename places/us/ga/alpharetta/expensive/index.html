In comparison to other places in this area, Alpharetta is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Alpharetta is $422,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $33,720 (8%) higher. It is roughly $205,300 (94%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $132,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Alpharetta is $1,451 per month. It is about $102 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $389 (36%) higher, and it is about $298 (25%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
