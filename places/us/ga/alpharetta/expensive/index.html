In comparison to other places in this area, Alpharetta is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Alpharetta's median home value is $422,800. It is about $33,720 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $205,300 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $132,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

In Alpharetta the median monthly rent is $1,451. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $102 (7%) higher.It is roughly $389 (36%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $298 (25%) higher.
