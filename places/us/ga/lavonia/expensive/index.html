Home prices

The median home price in Lavonia is $112,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $19,160 (20%) higher.It is about $105,300 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Sandy Springs is about $359,600 (76%) lower.

Rents

Lavonia's median rent is $593 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $139 (18%) lower.It is approximately $469 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $734 (55%) lower compared to the city of Sandy Springs.
