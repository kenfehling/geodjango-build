Home prices

The value of the median home in Lavonia is $112,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $19,160 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $105,300 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sandy Springs is about $359,600 (76%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lavonia is $593 a month. This is about $139 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $469 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Sandy Springs is roughly $734 (55%) lower.
