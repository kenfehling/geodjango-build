Home prices

The median home in Sugar Hill is valued at $232,500. This is roughly $18,560 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $15,000 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $57,900 (19%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Sugar Hill's median rent is $1,263 a month. This is about $70 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $201 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $110 (9%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
