Home prices

The median home in Hammond is valued at $159,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $25,920 (19%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $58,200 (26%) lower, and is roughly $72,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

The median rent in Hammond is $796 per month. This is about $47 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $266 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is roughly $202 (20%) lower.
