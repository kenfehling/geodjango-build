Home prices

Rents

Jonesboro's median rent is $545 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $51 (10%) higher.It is about $517 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $267 (32%) lower.
