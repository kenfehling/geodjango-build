In comparison to other places in this area, Jonesboro is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jonesboro is $79,500. This is about $5,740 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $138,000 (63%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $68,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Jonesboro the median rent is $545 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $51 (10%) higher. It is about $517 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $267 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
