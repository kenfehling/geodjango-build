Compared to its nearby areas, Crowley is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Home prices

The median home in Crowley costs $115,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $22,440 (24%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $102,200 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $58,700 (33%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

The median rent in Crowley is $754 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $118 (18%) higher. It is about $308 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $125 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge.
