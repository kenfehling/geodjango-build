Home prices

Rents

Franklin's median monthly rent is $744. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $10 (1%) lower. It is about $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Baton Rouge is approximately $135 (15%) lower.
