The median home in Franklin costs $79,200. It is approximately $16,640 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $138,300 (63%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $94,800 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Franklin is $744. It is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Baton Rouge is roughly $135 (15%) lower.
