Home prices

The median home price in Woodworth is $201,300. It is roughly $91,800 (83%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $16,200 (7%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $5,900 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Lafayette.

Rents

The median rent in Woodworth is $689 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Lafayette is approximately $201 (22%) lower.
