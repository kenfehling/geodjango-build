Compared to its surrounding areas, Livonia is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Baton Rouge, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Livonia is valued at $139,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $26,840 (23%) higher. It is approximately $78,200 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Baton Rouge is approximately $34,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

Livonia's median monthly rent is $678. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $21 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $384 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge it is approximately $201 (22%) lower.
