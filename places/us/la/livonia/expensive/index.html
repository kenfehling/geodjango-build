Compared to the surrounding areas, Livonia is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Baton Rouge, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Livonia's median home cost is $139,300. This is roughly $26,840 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $78,200 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $34,700 (19%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

In Livonia the median rent is $678 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $21 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $384 (36%) lower, and is about $201 (22%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.
