Home prices

The median home in Livonia costs $139,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $26,840 (23%) higher.It is roughly $78,200 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $34,700 (19%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

Livonia's median rent is $678 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $21 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $384 (36%) lower, and is roughly $201 (22%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.
