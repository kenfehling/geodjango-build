In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lafayette is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Baton Rouge, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette costs $195,400. This is approximately $23,880 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $22,100 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $21,400 (12%) higher compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

Lafayette's median rent is $890 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $47 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $172 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge it is about $11 (1%) higher.
