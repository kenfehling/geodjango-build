Home prices

The median home price in Lafayette is $195,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $23,880 (13%) higher.It is roughly $22,100 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge is roughly $21,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Lafayette the median monthly rent is $890. Compared to other places in this area this is about $47 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $172 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge it is roughly $11 (1%) higher.
