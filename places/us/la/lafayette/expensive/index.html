Home prices

The median home price in Lafayette is $195,400. It is about $23,880 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $22,100 (10%) lower, and is approximately $21,400 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

In Lafayette the median rent is $890 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $47 (5%) lower. It is about $172 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Baton Rouge it is about $11 (1%) higher.
