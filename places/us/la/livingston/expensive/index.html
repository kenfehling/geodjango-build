In comparison to the surrounding areas, Livingston is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

The median home in Livingston costs $151,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $16,580 (9%) lower. It is roughly $66,500 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is approximately $80,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Livingston the median monthly rent is $845. It is approximately $55 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $217 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is roughly $153 (15%) lower.
