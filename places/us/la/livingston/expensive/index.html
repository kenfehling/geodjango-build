Home prices

The median home in Livingston is valued at $151,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $16,580 (9%) lower. It is roughly $66,500 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is roughly $80,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

Livingston's median rent is $845 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $55 (6%) higher. It is approximately $217 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $153 (15%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.
