Home prices

The median home in Winnsboro costs $83,600. This is roughly $5,460 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $133,900 (61%) lower, and it is about $56,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Monroe.

Rents

In Winnsboro the median rent is $616 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $77 (14%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $446 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Monroe it is roughly $86 (12%) lower.
