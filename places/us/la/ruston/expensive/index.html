Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ruston is $169,600. This is approximately $21,600 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $47,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $21,500 (14%) higher compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Ruston the median monthly rent is $744. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $98 (15%) higher. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $68 (8%) lower.
