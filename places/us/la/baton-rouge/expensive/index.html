Compared to other places in this area, Baton Rouge is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Baton Rouge is $174,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,540 (5%) lower. It is about $43,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New Orleans is about $57,500 (24%) lower.

Rents

Baton Rouge's median monthly rent is $879. It is roughly $204 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $119 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.
