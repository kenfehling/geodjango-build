Home prices

The median home in Baton Rouge costs $174,000. It is about $9,540 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $43,500 (19%) lower, and it is about $57,500 (24%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baton Rouge is $879. It is roughly $204 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is approximately $119 (11%) lower.
