Compared to nearby areas, Baton Rouge is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Baton Rouge is $174,000. It is roughly $9,540 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $43,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is roughly $57,500 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baton Rouge is $879. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $204 (18%) lower. It is about $183 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans is about $119 (11%) lower.
