Rents

The median rent in Glenmora is $578 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $7 (1%) lower. It is roughly $484 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Lafayette it is roughly $312 (35%) lower.
