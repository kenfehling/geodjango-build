Home prices

The median home in Lake Charles costs $155,700. It is approximately $3,100 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $61,800 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Lafayette is about $39,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Charles the median rent is $816 per month. It is approximately $164 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $246 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lafayette is about $74 (8%) lower.
