Home prices

Rents

Lake Charles's median monthly rent is $816. It is roughly $164 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $246 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $74 (8%) lower compared to the city of Lafayette.
