Home prices

The cost of the median home in Arcadia is $83,300. It is about $7,020 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $134,200 (61%) lower, and is roughly $64,800 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Arcadia's median rent is $485 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $117 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $577 (54%) lower, and is roughly $327 (40%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
