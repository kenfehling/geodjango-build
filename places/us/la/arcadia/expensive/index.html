Home prices

The median home in Arcadia is valued at $83,300. This is roughly $7,020 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $134,200 (61%) lower, and it is roughly $64,800 (43%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Arcadia's median rent is $485 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $117 (19%) lower.It is roughly $577 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $327 (40%) lower.
