Home prices

The price of the median home in Ashland is $108,000. It is roughly $30,320 (39%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $109,500 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $40,100 (27%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Ashland the median monthly rent is $879. It is about $286 (48%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $183 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $67 (8%) higher.
