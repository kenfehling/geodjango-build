Home prices

The value of the median home in Ashland is $108,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $30,320 (39%) higher.It is roughly $109,500 (50%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $40,100 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Ashland's median rent is $879 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $286 (48%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $183 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $67 (8%) higher.
