Home prices

Rents

In Ashland the median monthly rent is $879. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $286 (48%) higher.It is approximately $183 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $67 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
