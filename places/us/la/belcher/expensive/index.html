Compared to the surrounding areas, Belcher is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Shreveport, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Belcher is valued at $137,500. This is about $20,760 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $80,000 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $10,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Belcher the median rent is $763 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $10 (1%) higher. It is roughly $299 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is approximately $49 (6%) lower.
