In comparison to its surrounding areas, Metairie is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New Orleans, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Metairie costs $236,900. It is about $20,280 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $19,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is approximately $5,400 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Metairie is $952 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $69 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $110 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is about $46 (4%) lower.
