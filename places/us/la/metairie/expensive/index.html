Home prices

The median home in Metairie costs $236,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $20,280 (9%) higher. It is about $19,400 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $5,400 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

In Metairie the median monthly rent is $952. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $69 (6%) lower. It is about $110 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $46 (4%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.
