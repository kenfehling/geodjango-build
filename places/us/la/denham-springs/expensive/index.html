Home prices

The cost of the median home in Denham Springs is $161,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $36,220 (18%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $55,900 (25%) lower, and is about $69,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

In Denham Springs the median rent is $853 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $161 (15%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $209 (19%) lower, and is about $145 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.
