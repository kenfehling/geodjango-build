Home prices

The cost of the median home in Henderson is $63,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $50,000 (44%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $154,100 (70%) lower, and it is roughly $110,600 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

In Henderson the median monthly rent is $668. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $65 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $394 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge it is roughly $211 (24%) lower.
