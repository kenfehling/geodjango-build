Home prices

The median home in West Monroe costs $133,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $43,700 (48%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $84,400 (38%) lower, and is approximately $6,500 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Monroe.

Rents

The median rent in West Monroe is $744 a month. This is roughly $33 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $318 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Monroe is approximately $42 (5%) higher.
