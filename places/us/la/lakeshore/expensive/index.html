Home prices

The median home in Lakeshore costs $123,200. This is about $14,400 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $94,300 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Monroe it is roughly $16,400 (11%) lower.

Rents

Lakeshore's median monthly rent is $1,013. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $252 (33%) higher. It is roughly $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Monroe is approximately $311 (44%) higher.
