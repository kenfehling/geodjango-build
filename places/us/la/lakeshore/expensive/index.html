Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeshore is $1,013. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $252 (33%) higher. It is approximately $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $311 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Monroe.
