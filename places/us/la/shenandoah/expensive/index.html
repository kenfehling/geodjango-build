Home prices

The price of the median home in Shenandoah is $261,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $2,660 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $44,400 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is about $30,400 (13%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Shenandoah is $1,104 per month. This is about $181 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $42 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $106 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of New Orleans.
