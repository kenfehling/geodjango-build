Rents

Shenandoah's median rent is $1,104 per month. It is roughly $181 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $42 (3%) higher, and is roughly $106 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of New Orleans.
