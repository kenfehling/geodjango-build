In comparison to its surrounding areas, Iowa is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Lafayette.

Home prices

The median home value in Iowa is $136,800. It is roughly $8,760 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $80,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Lafayette it is approximately $58,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

Iowa's median rent is $827 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $112 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and is roughly $63 (7%) lower compared to the city of Lafayette.
