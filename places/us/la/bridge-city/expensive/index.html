Rents

Bridge City's median monthly rent is $928. This is roughly $126 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $134 (12%) lower, and is roughly $70 (7%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.
