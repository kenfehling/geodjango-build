Compared to the surrounding areas, New Orleans is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home in New Orleans costs $231,500. This is about $52,033 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $14,000 (6%) higher compared to the rest of the nation.

Rents

New Orleans's median monthly rent is $998. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $40 (4%) higher.It is roughly $64 (6%) lower compared to the entire country.
