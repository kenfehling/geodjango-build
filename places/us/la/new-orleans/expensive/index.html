In comparison to its surrounding areas, New Orleans is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home price in New Orleans is $231,500. This is about $52,033 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $14,000 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole.

Rents

New Orleans's median rent is $998 per month. It is approximately $40 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $64 (6%) lower.
