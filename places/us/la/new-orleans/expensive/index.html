In comparison to other places in this area, New Orleans is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in New Orleans costs $231,500. This is about $52,033 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $14,000 (6%) higher.

Rents

New Orleans's median monthly rent is $998. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $40 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $64 (6%) lower.
