Home prices

Rents

Claiborne's median rent is $963 per month. This is approximately $229 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $99 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Monroe it is approximately $261 (37%) higher.
