Compared to nearby areas, Montgomery is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home value is $88,800. This is roughly $54,520 (38%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $128,700 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $59,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $723. This is about $73 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $339 (31%) lower, and is roughly $89 (10%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
