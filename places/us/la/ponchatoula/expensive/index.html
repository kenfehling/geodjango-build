Similar to other places in this area, it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

The median home in Ponchatoula is valued at $152,500. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $65,000 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans is roughly $79,000 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ponchatoula is $900. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $47 (5%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $162 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is about $98 (9%) lower.
