Home prices

The median home price in Inniswold is $270,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $13,100 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $53,100 (24%) higher, and it is roughly $39,100 (16%) higher compared to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

In Inniswold the median rent is $1,075 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $216 (16%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $13 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is roughly $77 (7%) higher.
