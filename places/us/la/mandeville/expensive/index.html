Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mandeville is $288,900. It is approximately $73,960 (34%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $71,400 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is about $57,400 (24%) higher.

Rents

Mandeville's median monthly rent is $1,133. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $34 (3%) higher.It is about $71 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is approximately $135 (13%) higher.
