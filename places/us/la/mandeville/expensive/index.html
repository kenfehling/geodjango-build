Home prices

The median home in Mandeville is valued at $288,900. It is roughly $73,960 (34%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $71,400 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is approximately $57,400 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mandeville is $1,133 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $34 (3%) higher.It is about $71 (6%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans is roughly $135 (13%) higher.
