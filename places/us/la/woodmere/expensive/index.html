Home prices

The median home price in Woodmere is $133,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $39,120 (22%) lower. It is roughly $84,300 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $98,300 (42%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Woodmere is $1,088. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $20 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $26 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is approximately $90 (9%) higher.
