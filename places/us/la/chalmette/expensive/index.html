Home prices

Chalmette's median home cost is $152,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $5,760 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $64,600 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is approximately $78,600 (33%) lower.

Rents

Chalmette's median monthly rent is $960. This is about $39 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $102 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans is about $38 (3%) lower.
