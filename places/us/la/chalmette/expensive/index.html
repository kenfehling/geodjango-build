Much like its surrounding areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of New Orleans however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Chalmette's median home cost is $152,900. It is roughly $5,760 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $64,600 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $78,600 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

Chalmette's median rent is $960 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $39 (4%) higher. It is about $102 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is about $38 (3%) lower.
