Home prices

The median home in Bastrop costs $72,200. It is roughly $33,580 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $145,300 (66%) lower, and compared to the city of Monroe it is about $67,400 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bastrop is $559 per month. It is approximately $208 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $503 (47%) lower, and it is about $143 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Monroe.
