Compared to its surrounding areas, Bastrop is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Monroe.

Home prices

The median home price in Bastrop is $72,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $33,580 (31%) lower. It is approximately $145,300 (66%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $67,400 (48%) lower compared to the city of Monroe.

Rents

The median rent in Bastrop is $559 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $208 (27%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $503 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Monroe is roughly $143 (20%) lower.
