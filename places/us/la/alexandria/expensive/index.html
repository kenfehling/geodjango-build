Home prices

The median home in Alexandria costs $148,900. This is approximately $14,540 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $68,600 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $46,500 (23%) lower compared to the city of Lafayette.

Rents

The median rent in Alexandria is $831 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $99 (13%) higher. It is roughly $231 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $59 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Lafayette.
