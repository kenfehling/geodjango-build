Compared to its surrounding areas, Alexandria is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Lafayette, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Alexandria's median home cost is $148,900. This is approximately $14,540 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $68,600 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Lafayette is roughly $46,500 (23%) lower.

Rents

Alexandria's median monthly rent is $831. It is roughly $99 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $231 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Lafayette it is about $59 (6%) lower.
