Home prices

The value of the median home in Tallulah is $73,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $42,260 (36%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $143,600 (66%) lower, and it is roughly $16,800 (18%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

In Tallulah the median monthly rent is $604. It is about $100 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $458 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson is approximately $243 (28%) lower.
