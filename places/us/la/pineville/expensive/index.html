Home prices

The price of the median home in Pineville is $157,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $41,900 (36%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $60,400 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $38,300 (19%) lower compared to the city of Lafayette.

Rents

Pineville's median monthly rent is $729. This is roughly $20 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Lafayette it is about $161 (18%) lower.
