In comparison to the surrounding areas, Benton is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The median home cost in Benton is $146,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $19,700 (15%) higher. It is roughly $71,000 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $1,600 (1%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Benton's median monthly rent is $698. It is approximately $95 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $364 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $114 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
