Home prices

The price of the median home in Benton is $146,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $19,700 (15%) higher. It is roughly $71,000 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $1,600 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Benton the median monthly rent is $698. It is approximately $95 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $364 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $114 (14%) lower.
