Home prices

The cost of the median home in Benton is $146,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $19,700 (15%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $71,000 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is approximately $1,600 (1%) lower.

Rents

Benton's median rent is $698 per month. This is about $95 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $364 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $114 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
