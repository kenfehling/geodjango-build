Home prices

The median home value in Bossier City is $163,500. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $54,000 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is approximately $15,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

Bossier City's median monthly rent is $955. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $11 (1%) higher. It is approximately $107 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is approximately $143 (17%) higher.
