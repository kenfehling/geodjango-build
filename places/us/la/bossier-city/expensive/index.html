Home prices

The price of the median home in Bossier City is $163,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $54,000 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is about $15,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Bossier City the median monthly rent is $955. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $11 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $107 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is about $143 (17%) higher.
