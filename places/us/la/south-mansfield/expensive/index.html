The median home value in South Mansfield is $75,300. It is about $13,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $142,200 (65%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $72,800 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in South Mansfield is $341 per month. It is approximately $259 (43%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $721 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $471 (58%) lower.
