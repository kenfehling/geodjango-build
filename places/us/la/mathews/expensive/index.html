Home prices

The median home in Mathews costs $198,100. This is approximately $55,720 (39%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $19,400 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $33,400 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mathews is $851. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $30 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $211 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is roughly $147 (14%) lower.
