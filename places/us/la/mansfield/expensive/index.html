In comparison to other places in this area, Mansfield is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Shreveport, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mansfield is $80,200. This is approximately $49,580 (38%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,300 (63%) lower, and is about $67,900 (45%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in Mansfield is $495 per month. It is roughly $159 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $567 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Shreveport is approximately $317 (39%) lower.
