Home prices

The value of the median home in Rayne is $114,800. This is about $11,780 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $102,700 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge is approximately $59,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Rayne the median monthly rent is $596. It is approximately $149 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $466 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Baton Rouge it is approximately $283 (32%) lower.
