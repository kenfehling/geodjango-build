Home prices

The median home in Atlanta is valued at $75,000. It is approximately $16,800 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $142,500 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $73,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atlanta is $563. It is about $107 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $499 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $249 (30%) lower.
