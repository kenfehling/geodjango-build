Home prices

The median home value in Atlanta is $75,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16,800 (18%) lower.It is about $142,500 (65%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Shreveport is approximately $73,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median rent is $563 a month. It is roughly $107 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $499 (46%) lower, and it is roughly $249 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
