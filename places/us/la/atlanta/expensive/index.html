Home prices

The price of the median home in Atlanta is $75,000. It is approximately $16,800 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $142,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $73,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Atlanta is $563 a month. It is roughly $107 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $499 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is about $249 (30%) lower.
