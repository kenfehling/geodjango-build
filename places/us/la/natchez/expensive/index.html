Compared to other places in this area, Natchez is a lot more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The median home in Natchez is valued at $250,000. This is approximately $126,340 (102%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $32,500 (14%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Shreveport is approximately $101,900 (68%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Natchez is $588. It is about $144 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $474 (44%) lower, and is roughly $224 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
